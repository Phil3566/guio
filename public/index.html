Quick Reference

Cook & Reheat

Reheat food: Sensor Reheat → Start — auto-detects time. Don't open door until 2 beeps.
Cook (manual): enter time on 0–9 → Start — full power by default.
Lower power: Power Level press repeatedly (10 levels: PL10 highest → PL1 lowest), then enter time → Start — common: PL7 reheat, PL6 dense food, PL3 defrost.
Add 30 sec: Quick 30 — each press = +30 sec. Works while cooking.
Keep warm: Keep Warm → enter min (max 30) → Start

Clock & Timer

Button Guide

Cooking Buttons

Sensor Reheat — reheats food automatically. Press once, then Start. Don't open door until 2 beeps.
Sensor Cook — cooks by food type. Press until code 1–12 shows, then Start. See Quick Reference for codes.
Popcorn — pops microwave popcorn. Press 1x for 3.5 oz, 2x for 3 oz, 3x for 1.75 oz, then Start.
Power Level — sets cooking power. Press repeatedly to cycle 10 levels: PL10 (high) down to PL1 (low). Then enter time.
Turbo Defrost — fast defrost by weight. Enter lbs (up to 6), then Start. Flip food when it beeps twice.
Keep Warm — holds food warm after cooking. Enter minutes (up to 30), then Start. Cannot combine with sensor.
Quick 30 — quick start at full power. Each press adds 30 sec. Also works while already cooking.

Adjustment Buttons

More — increases sensor cooking time. Press before Start.
Less — decreases sensor cooking time. Press before Start.
0–9 — number pad. Enter cook time, weight, or food codes.

Clock & Timer

Timer — kitchen countdown timer. No heat. Press Timer, enter time, then Start.
Clock Set — sets time of day. Press once, enter time on number pad, press again to confirm. 12-hour format.

Controls

Start — starts any cooking program. Also used to lock child lock (press 3 times quickly).
Stop / Reset — press once to pause (Start to resume). Press twice to cancel everything. Unlocks child lock (press 3x).
Door Release — opens the door. Also pauses cooking automatically.

Care & Info

Cleaning

Before cleaning: unplug oven. Leave door open.
Outside: damp cloth. Don't let water seep into vents.
Inside: damp cloth after each use. Mild detergent OK. No harsh cleaners or abrasives.
Door: soft dry cloth. Steam on door is normal. Do not remove inner heat barrier film.
Cavity floor: mild detergent, water, or window cleaner. Dry.
Wave guide cover: do not remove. Keep clean like the inside.
Control panel: soft dry cloth. No harsh detergent. Peel off shipping film if still on.
Glass tray: warm soapy water or dishwasher.
Roller ring: mild soapy water or dishwasher. Keep clean to avoid noise.
Important: after use, wipe dry all surfaces including vent openings, seams, and under glass tray. Food residue causes arcing and damage.

Troubleshooting

TV interference: normal, like a mixer or vacuum. Not a fault.
Steam on door / warm air from vents: normal. Wipe door dry after use.
Won't turn on: unplug, wait 10 sec, re-plug. Check fuse / breaker. Try another outlet.
Won't start cooking: close door firmly. Press Start after programming. Press Stop/Reset first to clear old program.
Glass tray wobbles: reposition on roller ring. Check for food underneath.
Noise from glass tray: roller ring or oven bottom is dirty. Clean both.
Lock icon on display: child lock is on. Press Stop/Reset x3 to unlock.
