Is there a monthly fee to use the guide?
No. The guide is free to use forever. You're paying for the physical sticker — the AI guide it links to has no subscription, no login, and no expiration.
What if my question isn't in the guide?
The AI chat can answer questions in real time. It's been trained on the official Frameo user manual and thousands of customer experiences. Just type your question and get an answer.
Will the sticker damage my frame?
No. It uses repositionable matte-finish adhesive that peels off cleanly without leaving residue. Safe on plastic, metal, and wood surfaces.
Can I buy this as a gift along with the frame?
Yes — that's exactly what it's designed for. Order the sticker with the frame and stick it on the back before wrapping. When they open the gift, the guide is already there.
